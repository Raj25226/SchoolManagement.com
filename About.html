ABOUT




Schooling

The School houses about 950 students at present and the classes are taken in 1 three storied building (H.S. Block) and 1 two storied building (Kishalaya) for Primary classes. Science Labs., Computer Labs and other activity classes are taken in a newly built 3 storied building which has an Auditorium in its ground floor. The School has a mission of maintaining a Plastic free campus and building a park-like a garden for the tenqer minds of the Institution.


Hostel

The hostel houses about 500 students at present and the rooms are in 1 three storied building (H.S. Block) and 1 two storied building (Kishalaya) for Primary students. The hostel has a mission of maintaining a Plastic free campus and building a park-like a garden for the tenqer minds of the Institution.
